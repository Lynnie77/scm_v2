Mission

About Us

Sacred Connections Ministry is a 501(c)(3) non-profit that is committed to our congregation living in their highest selves. We strive to achieve this elevated spiritual self through guided meditation, professional workshops, visualization and the healing powers of a spiritual sacrament. Through this holy work, an individual can experience alignment on a conscious, subconscious and super conscious level which can foster actualization of this higher self.

We believe that people are most effective in their lives at this deep level of awareness. To attain this higher connection, one must be free of anger and pain to live within this vibration. The spiritual Sacrament of Cannabis aids and supports the attainment of this vibration through its healing properties as an herbal entheogen in its various forms, such as holy anointing oil, topical salves, and spiritual salts for bathing. We believe that these work best in tandem with the ancient healing powers of crystals, chakra-based artwork and sacred geometry to promote physical, emotional and spiritual well-being and self-awareness. Self-awareness grants us the ability to develop ourselves so we are better equipped to work with and serve others.

Through donations, Sacred Connections Ministry will be able to better serve our community through generous contributions, education, opportunities for community involvement and small local gatherings. Our goal to serve our congregation and inspire them to form sacred connections with self and others.
